The Partnership Maturity Scale

Three stages of human-AI collaboration competency. The same skills apply across domains.

LEVELS 1-3

Learning to Speak (The Foundation)

Core Competency: Translating intent into dialogue the AI can act on.

VibeCoding

  • • Articulating vague ideas as actionable prompts
  • • Describing outcomes, not implementation
  • • Providing context that prevents hallucination

AEIS Governance

  • • Framing audit questions AI can answer
  • • Specifying evidence requirements clearly
  • • Providing policy context that constrains

Success: Output matches intent on first or second attempt.

LEVELS 4-6

Learning to Listen (Pre-Flight)

Core Competency: Recognizing when to pause and consult—reading the signals. Cleared for takeoff.

VibeCoding

  • • Detecting context rot before corruption
  • • Recognizing hallucinated schemas/APIs
  • • Reading AI confidence signals

AEIS Governance

  • • Detecting provenance breaks
  • • Recognizing scores that don't match intuition
  • • Reading uncertainty indicators

Success: Catch problems BEFORE they propagate. You're cleared for takeoff.

LEVELS 7-10

Learning to Partner (Flight ✈)

Core Competency: Genuine collaborative problem-solving—outcomes neither could achieve alone.

VibeCoding

  • • Multi-agent orchestration with oversight
  • • Security hardening through dialogue
  • • Creative outcomes that surprise both

AEIS Governance

  • • H∞P continuous governance as conversation
  • • Audit defense through dialogue trails
  • • Discovery at scales no team could achieve

Success: Outcome better than either could produce alone.
