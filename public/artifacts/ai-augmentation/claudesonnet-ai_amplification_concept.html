Viral Strategy: Making This Vision Unstoppable

1

Story-First Approach

Share specific transformation stories, not abstract concepts. "Meet Sarah, former accountant turned climate architect..."

2

Visual Transformation

Before/after imagery showing people in upgraded roles solving bigger problems. Make the upgrade obvious and inspiring.

3

Challenge the Narrative

Reframe every "AI replaced X jobs" headline with "AI freed X people to solve Y crisis." Make replacement stories feel outdated.

4

Community Building

Create spaces for people to envision their own amplified roles. "What would you do if AI handled your routine tasks?"

5

Corporate Challenge

Challenge companies: "Show us how you're amplifying humans, not just replacing them." Create public accountability.

6

Success Amplification

Celebrate every real example. Create awards for "Human Amplification" rather than just efficiency gains.

Viral Content Templates

  • Twitter/X: "THREAD: What if instead of 'AI replaced 1000 cashiers' we saw 'AI freed 1000 people to become community wellbeing coordinators' 🧵"
  • LinkedIn: "The best companies aren't replacing humans with AI. They're using AI to help humans solve problems we never thought possible."
  • TikTok: "POV: AI doesn't take your job, it upgrades it" (show dramatic before/after transformation)
  • Instagram: Split-screen carousel posts showing job transformations with inspirational quotes
