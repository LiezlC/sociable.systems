Go‑Viral Strategy (ethical edition)

  1. Own the contrast. Lead with the replace → redeploy flip. Visual before verbal. Keep it punchy.
  2. Package a tiny tool. Ship this page (or a fork) with your brand; invite screenshots of slider settings that unlocked breakthroughs.
  3. Make it personal. Ask, “What was your oldest job title? What’s its coolest redeployed version?” Thread the replies.
  4. Case‑drop weekly. Publish one real‑pattern and one hypothetical every week for 6 weeks. End each with a 2‑minute teardown.
  5. Ally up. Partner with a union, a bootcamp, and an open‑source maintainer to co‑author a "Redeploy Playbook" PDF. Free, no email wall.
  6. Instrument the meme. Use the hook generator to iterate; A/B test headline + first image in 24h sprints. Retire the duds quickly.

Golden rule: celebrate capability, not casualties.

P.S. If you actually redeploy people into meaningful work, tell that story loudly. The future notices.
